## Visualizing Government Debt

The following charts visualize government debt from 2007 to 2024. Data was taken from [OECD](https://data.oecd.org/).

The first visualization uses circles to show the GDP level of a given country in a given year. It uses several pages to show GDP changes over time in various countries. As the title suggests, countries' GDP generally increased over time, but experienced a dip in 2022.
